|
A paramedic provides emergency medical care to people who are injured or
unwell. They respond quickly to 999 calls and are often the first at the scene
of an accident. Paramedics assess patients, perform life-saving procedures,
and transport them to hospitals. The role is physically demanding, emotionally
intense, and requires quick decision-making under pressure.
Environmental Scientist
An environmental scientist studies the natural world and works to protect it.
They analyse pollution levels, test soil and water, and generate reports to
inform laws or policies. Their work often helps reduce environmental damage
and promote sustainability. Environmental scientists may work outdoors, in
labs or advise governments and industries.
A teacher helps pupils learn and grow. They plan lessons, explain new ideas,
and support pupils academically and emotionally. Teaching also involves
working closely with parents and other staff. A good teacher inspires curiosity,
builds confidence, and creates a positive classroom environment. Though
challenging, it is a job that brings long-term rewards and purpose.
