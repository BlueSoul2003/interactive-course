Part 2: Answering "Why"

第二部分：回答“为什么” (Why)

When someone asks "Why...", we usually answer with "Because...".

当有人问“Why（为什么）...”时，我们通常用“Because（因为）...”来回答。

1. Question (问题): "Why are you wearing a raincoat?" 为什么你穿着雨衣？

2. Question (问题): "Why are you running?" 你为什么在跑步？

3. Question (问题): "Why is the baby crying?" 为什么宝宝在哭？

4. Question (问题): "Why are you going to bed early?" 你为什么这么早去睡觉？

Part 4: Expressing Feelings

第四部分：表达感受

When asked "How did you feel?", we reply with "I felt..." (past tense).

当被问到“你当时感觉怎么样？”时，我们用“I felt...（我感觉...）”回答（过去式）。

1. Scenario (情境): You played in the snow without a jacket. Someone asks: "How did you feel?"

你不穿外套在雪地里玩。有人问：“你当时感觉怎么样？”

2. Scenario (情境): You won first prize in a drawing competition.

你在画画比赛中得了一等奖。

3. Scenario (情境): You watched a scary movie in the dark.

你在黑暗中看了一部恐怖电影。

4. Scenario (情境): You lost your favorite toy at the park.

你在公园里弄丢了最喜欢的玩具。

Grammar Rule: Two Actions in the Past

语法规则：过去的两个动作

When two things happen in the past, one is usually a LONG action and one is a SHORT action that interrupts it.

当过去发生两件事时，通常一个是长动作，另一个是突然发生并打断它的短动作。

  • 1. Long Action (长动作): Use was/were + -ing. We often use "while" before it.
    Example: ... while she was cooking. (当时她正在做饭)
  • 2. Short Action (短动作): Use simple past (ends in -ed or irregular). We often use "when" before it.
    Example: ... when the phone rang. (这时电话响了)
