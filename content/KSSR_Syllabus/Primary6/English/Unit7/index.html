TRK 09Read about film musicFINAL MIX

What is one main job of music in a film?

电影音乐的主要作用之一是什么？

Film composers use music to create mood. A slow melody may make a scene feel sad, while a fast rhythm can make an action scene exciting.

Look at the first sentence.

Music changes how a scene feels.
