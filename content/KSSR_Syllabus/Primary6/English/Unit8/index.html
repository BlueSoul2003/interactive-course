IThe Rose and the CactusCLOZE

The proud rose ___ about her beauty.

选择最适合故事语境的动词。

The rose admired her petals and often spoke proudly. She ___ about her beauty, but laughed at the plain cactus nearby.

The rose spoke too proudly.

The word begins with b.

IIFind the lessonMORAL

What can we learn from the rose and cactus?

这个故事告诉我们什么道理？

During the dry season, the rose became weak. The cactus still had water and kindly shared it with her.

The plain cactus was kind and useful.

Looks do not show a person's true value.

VIIIThe Golden AppleLEGEND

Why did the goddesses want the Golden Apple?

女神们为什么想得到 Golden Apple？

The apple carried the words "For the fairest." Three goddesses each believed it belonged to her, so they asked Paris to decide.

Look at the words written on the apple.

Fairest means most beautiful.
