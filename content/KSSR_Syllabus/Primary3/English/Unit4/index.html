There are four seasons🌸 季節 in a year: spring, summer, autumn and winter. In spring, flowers bloom🌷 開花 and the weather becomes warmer. People often go for walks in the park. Summer is the hottest🔥 最熱的 season. Many children enjoy swimming or eating ice cream. In autumn🍁 秋天, the leaves turn red, orange and yellow. The weather gets cooler and windier. Winter is the coldest season. Sometimes it snows❄️ 下雪, and people wear thick clothes🧥 厚衣服 to stay warm.
一年有四個季節：春、夏、秋、冬。在春天，花朵綻放，天氣變暖。人們常去公園散步。夏天是最熱的季節。許多小孩喜歡游泳或吃冰淇淋。在秋天，樹葉變成紅色、橘色和黃色。天氣變得比較涼爽，風也比較大。冬天是最冷的季節。有時候會下雪，人們穿厚衣服保暖。

A February

This is the second and shortest month of the year with 28 days, except during leap years when it has 29 days. Leap year occurs every four years. The next leap year will be in 2028.

這是一年中第二個也是最短的月份，有28天。除了一年有29天的「閏年」。閏年每四年發生一次。下一次閏年是2028年。

B June

This month is named after Goddess Juno. In Malaysia, this is the month where Sarawakians celebrate the vibrant Harvesting Festival yearly for three days. It is a festival of thanksgiving.

這個月以朱諾女神命名。在馬來西亞，砂拉越人每年這個月都會慶祝為期三天的豐收節。這是一個感恩豐收的節日。

C December

This is the twelfth and final month. Before the pandemic, this was the month where students had a month long holiday. Every year on the 25th of this month, Christmas is celebrated.

這是第十二個也是最後一個月。在疫情前，學生這個月會放長達一個月的假。每年這個月的25日都會慶祝聖誕節。
