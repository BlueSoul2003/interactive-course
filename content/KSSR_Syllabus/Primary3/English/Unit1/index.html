A. Maya

Maya has got short, brown hair and bright blue eyes. She has a sprinkle of freckles on her nose. Maya can make amazing things with clay and paint, and she loves doing different kinds of arts and crafts. She can't, however, play football very well because she finds it hard to kick the ball in the right direction.

Maya 有著棕色短髮和明亮的藍色眼睛。她的鼻子上散落著一些雀斑。Maya 可以用黏土和顏料做出令人驚嘆的東西，她喜歡做各種不同的美術和手工藝。然而，她不太會踢足球，因為她覺得很難把球踢向正確的方向。
🏃‍♀️

B. Zara

Zara has got long, dark hair that she keeps tied in a ponytail. She is quite tall for her age and has very strong legs. Zara can run incredibly fast and is great at scoring goals during football games. She can't, however, play the piano because her fingers get all mixed up on the keys.

Zara 有著深色的長髮，她總是綁著馬尾。在同齡人中她算相當高，並且有著非常強壯的雙腿。Zara 跑得不可思議地快，在足球比賽中很擅長進球。然而，她不會彈鋼琴，因為她的手指會在琴鍵上打結。
👦🏼

C. Leo

Leo has got bright, curly blonde hair and wears shiny blue glasses. He always has a big smile on his face, which makes his eyes crinkle at the corners. Leo can beat anyone at a game of chess and can finish a difficult puzzle in no time. He can't, however, stay on a skateboard without falling over.

Leo 有著明亮的淡黃色捲髮，戴著閃亮的藍色眼鏡。他的臉上總是掛著大大的笑容，這讓他的眼角都瞇起了皺紋。Leo 可以在西洋棋比賽中擊敗任何人，並且能立刻完成困難的拼圖。然而，他沒辦法站在滑板上不摔倒。
