(A) Read the texts about three different food types. Then, assign each letter
to the statement that best represents it.
Dairy foods are made from milk, which comes from animals like cows
or goats. This food group includes milk, cheese, yoghurt, butter and
cream. People often have dairy during breakfast or as snacks. Dairy
is important because it gives our bodies calcium to help build strong
bones and teeth.
Meat comes from animals such as chickens, cows and fish. Some
[5 |] common types of meat are chicken wings, beef burgers, lamb chops
and grilled fish. Meat is an important part of many meals around the
world. It gives us protein, which helps our bodies grow strong and
repair themselves.
Legumes are a type of plant that grows in pods. Some examples
are beans, lentils, chickpeas and peas. They're often used in soups,
stews and vegetarian meals. Legumes are full of fibre and protein,
making them a healthy and filling choice for many people.

Cheeseburger.
Cheeseburgers are yummy sandwiches that come from America.
(23) Inside, there's a cooked beef patty, which is like a flat, round piece
of meat. And of course, there's cheese! It's often melted on top of
the patty. You can add other things too, like lettuce, tomato, onions,
and sauces like ketchup or mustard.
Crepes are like very thin pancakes, and they come from France.
[8 |] They're made with flour, eggs, milk, and butter, and they're cooked
on a flat pan. You can fill them with sweet things, like chocolate, fruit
or jam. Or, you can put savoury things in them, like cheese, ham or
vegetables. They are delicious for breakfast, lunch or even dessert!
Souvlaki is a tasty Greek dish. I's made of small pieces of meat,
like pork, that are grilled on a stick called a skewer. Sometimes they
add vegetables too! You can eat it right off the skewer, or wrapped
in a soft bread called pita. They often put lemon, sauces, tomatoes,
onions and fried potatoes with it.
