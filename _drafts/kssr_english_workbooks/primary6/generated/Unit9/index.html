3 Rainy Days Are Better Than Sunny Days
Some people love sunny days because they can play outside, go to the beach, or
have a picnic. However, I think rainy days are better.
Rainy days-are peaceful and calm. The sound of raindrops can be very relaxing.
On rainy days, I like to stay inside and read my favourite books or draw pictures.
Sometimes, I jump in puddles and splash around, which is really fun!
Rainy weather also helps plants grow and keeps the earth fresh. Without rain,
the trees and flowers would not be healthy. Plus, rainy days give us a chance to
slow down and rest. :
Even though sunny days are nice, rainy days have their own special magic.
